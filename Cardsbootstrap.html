KGF Chapter 3

Coming 2025

KGF Chapter 3 is confirmed and is slated for a 2025 release. The film will continue the story of Rocky, played by Yash, and is expected to explore his life between 1978-1981, which was previously omitted. Prashanth Neel, the director, has confirmed the script is ready, but his commitments to other projects, including Salaar and a film with Jr. NTR.
